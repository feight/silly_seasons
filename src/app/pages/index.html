Synopsis

Silly Seasons is an animated CG comedy featuring the escapades of four girls, Daisy, Summer, Ginger and Snowflake, who all live in the town of Sillyville with a supporting cast of offbeat animals and flowers. Â The 26 x 24' series is a lighthearted and irreverent take on relationships and life in general; the show is aimed at 8-12 y.o. girls.

The four girls are the only humans in the series as well as the only kids. All the other characters - anthropomorphic animals and plants - are adults. This forms the foundation for comedy, as the series parodies adult behavior and attitudes towards kids in a fun and humorous way.
